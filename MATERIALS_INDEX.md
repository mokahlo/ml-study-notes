## üí° Study Tips by Material Type

### When Reading PDFs
- Take handwritten notes
- Highlight key formulas and definitions
- Draw diagrams and examples
- Write mini-explanations in your own words

### When Running Notebooks
- Execute cells one by one
- Modify parameters and observe changes
- Try variations on the examples
- Add your own print statements to understand flow

### When Working Practice Problems
- Solve without looking at solutions first
- Check your work carefully
- Understand why solutions work
- Redo difficult problems

### When Using Concept Review
- Read explanations thoroughly
- Test yourself on definitions
- Draw concepts from memory
- Explain concepts aloud
